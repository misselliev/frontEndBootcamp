    How to make a PBJ sandwich

  1. Get two pieces of bread, butter, jelly and PB.
  2. Toast two breads with butter
  3. Get some peanut butter in one bread
  4. Spread jelly on the other bread
  5. Place the PB bread with the PB facing down the jelly
  6. Enjoy!
